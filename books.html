Books

  • Disparate Measures: The Intersectional Economics of Women in STEM Work (with Mary A. Armstrong). MIT Press, 2024.

    An intersectional economic analysis of the experiences of women in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, examining how gender intersects with race, sexuality, and other identities to shape careers in STEM work.

    Publisher

  • Women in the Workforce: What Everyone Needs to Know (with Laura M. Argys). Oxford University Press, 2022.

    An accessible, question-and-answer overview of women's changing role in the labor market—covering the gender wage gap, occupational segregation, work and family, and the policies that shape women's economic lives.

    Publisher

  • The Oxford Handbook of Women and the Economy (co-editor, with Laura M. Argys and Saul D. Hoffman). Oxford University Press, 2018.

    An edited volume bringing together leading scholars to survey the economics of women and gender, from labor supply and the family to health, discrimination, and public policy.

    Publisher

Textbook

  • Women and the Economy: Family, Work, and Pay (with Saul D. Hoffman and Laura M. Argys).

    A widely used undergraduate textbook on the economics of gender, now in its fifth edition, covering women's labor supply, the wage gap, family economics, discrimination, and the policies that affect women's economic outcomes.

    • 5th edition. Bloomsbury Press, 2024.

    • 4th edition. Macmillan–Red Globe Press, 2021 (with Saul D. Hoffman).

    • 3rd edition. Palgrave Macmillan, 2015 (with Saul D. Hoffman).

    • 2nd edition. Addison-Wesley, 2010 (with Saul D. Hoffman).

    • 1st edition. Addison-Wesley, 2005 (with Saul D. Hoffman).
